02 / About talking to you

I valued our conversations.

I really liked talking to you. You never disturbed me. In fact, you were one of the few people I could genuinely talk to and understand. I didn't say this much, but I valued our conversations.

03 / The real issue

This is something I have been trying to understand.

There's something about me that I've been trying to understand and fix. When I start caring about someone, my mind begins to react too strongly to small things. A late reply... a sudden silence... a conversation ending in the middle... These are normal things, but my mind doesn't treat them normally. It keeps thinking about them again and again, even when I don't want to.

04 / What it does to me

It quietly takes away focus.

Because of that, I start losing focus. It drains my energy, and even when I try to think about my life, my mind keeps going back to the same thoughts. I want to talk, but at the same time, I know how much it can affect me.

05 / My life right now

I am trying to build myself carefully.

Right now, I'm trying to build myself. I want to work on my future, improve my situation, take care of my health, my sleep, and become more stable. I don't have unlimited time or resources, so I have to be careful where I invest my energy.

06 / The decision

That is why I stepped back.

So I stepped back. Not because you did something wrong. Not because I didn't like talking to you. I stepped back because I couldn't manage my own mind properly. I didn't want to keep talking while struggling internally every day.

07 / Answer to your question

You are not the problem.

You once asked me what was wrong in you so you could improve. Honestly... there is nothing wrong with you. This was never about you being a problem. The real issue is how my mind reacts, how I handle attachment, and how I deal with small uncertainties. So please don't think you need to change yourself because of this. You are not the problem.

08 / Respect and care

I wanted to say this gently.

You're a good person, and I respect you. I know life can already be heavy sometimes, so I didn't want to add more weight to it. That's why I chose to say this in a calm and clear way.
